PAL - Human-in-the-Loop ML for behaviour change(2020)

PAL - Assisting Habit formation with Machine Learning ¶

Supervisor - Mina Khan

Members - Advait Rane, P Srivatsa

I am working on the Project PAL in the Fluid Interfaces Group at MIT Media Lab for my undergraduate thesis.

We are working on human-in-the-loop, sample-efficient Reinforcement Learning to assist users with context-specific interventions for habit formation. To do this in an interpretable manner, we are using attention models like Transformers to model user behaviour sequences and contextual bandits coupled with human guidance and bayesian estimation.
