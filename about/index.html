About me ¶

I’m a Masters student studying Computer Science at USC Viterbi. I’m broadly interested in Human-Centered ML applications and their ethical implications. More specifically I’m interested in fairness and bias in ML, Interpretability, and Privacy in ML applications.

I completed my B.E. in Computer Science from BITS Goa, India. I worked on my undergraduate thesis at MIT Media Lab, with the Fluid Interfaces Group in 2020 as a part of the Project PAL.

In my free time I enjoy playing basketball and reading fiction.
